techunifi LLC and OUS Capital Announce Strategic Partnership to Drive Innovation and Growth in energy savings as a service (ESaaS) sector.
High Point, NC – techunifi LLC, a leading technology solutions provider, and Onsite Utility Services (OUS) Capital, LLC, a prominent investment firm, today announced a strategic partnership aimed at driving innovation and growth in the technology and energy savings as a service (ESaaS) sector.
The partnership will leverage the strengths and expertise of both companies to create new opportunities for expansion and enhance ESaaS - energy as a service and technology upgrades in areas such as hospitality, MDU, assisted living, pharmacies, schools and city government throughout the US.
Under the agreement, OUS Capital will provide techunifi LLC’s clients with support for research and improvement for their energy infrastructure without incurring any debt or capital expense, and enabling them to accelerate the energy savings plus removing all maintenance & warranty expense from their OpEx. techunifi LLC will also gain access to OUS Capital's extensive network of industry contacts and resources, providing valuable market insights and opportunities for strategic partnerships including but not limited to, low cost LED lighting, smart thermostats, EV chargers, and other cloud services.
"We are thrilled to announce this partnership with OUS Capital," said Rajesh Patel, CEO of techunifi LLC. "Their expertise in the energy as a service industry and commitment to innovation make them the ideal partner for us as we continue to expand our offerings and drive growth in our business."
"We are excited to work with techunifi LLC to help drive innovation and growth in their clients’ ESaaS energy sector," said Fritz Kreiss, Founder of OUS Capital. "This partnership will enable us to collaborate with one of the industry's most innovative and dynamic companies and create new opportunities for growth and success." The partnership is expected to have a significant impact on the technology sector, as techunifi LLC and OUS Capital work together to bring new ESaaS innovations and services to market. Both companies are committed to driving innovation and creating new opportunities for growth in the technology industry, and this partnership will enable them to achieve these goals more effectively and efficiently.

About techunifi LLC: techunifi LLC is a leading technology solutions provider, offering a wide range of services to clients across various industries. The company is known for its innovative approach to technology and its commitment to delivering the highest level of optimization and robust technology solutions for commercial IT and technology clients, such as hospitality, hospitals, MDUs, assisted living, corporate offices, schools, etc.

About OUS Capital: OUS Capital serves as a nationwide energy efficiency and renewable energy integrator. Through our energy savings as a service funding platform, we allow our customers to pursue energy efficiency, water conservation, and renewable energy solutions as an off balance sheet transaction, with no capital expense or debt, allowing them to instead utilize their resources into their core operations. OUS Capital provides turnkey solutions in the areas of HVAC, power optimization, refrigeration, lighting, water conservation, resiliency and clean power generation.

Techunifi LLC Chosen as Low Voltage and Technology Vendor for Dual Brand Hilton Hotel Developed by Premier Petroleum Hospitality
techunifi LLC, a leading technology solutions provider, is proud to announce its selection as the low voltage and technology vendor for the upcoming Dual Brand Hilton Hotel developed by Premier Petroleum. The new hotel, located at Convention Center Concourse, will feature a state-of-the-art technology infrastructure designed to enhance the guest experience.
"We are pleased to be chosen as the low voltage and technology vendor for the Dual Brand Hilton Hotel project," said Rajesh Patel, CEO of techunifi LLC. “Our brand expertise will ensure that the hotel's technology infrastructure is fully brand compliant and provides guests with a seamless, convenient and user-friendly experience." techunifi LLC will be responsible for the design, and installation of the hotel's low voltage and technology infrastructure. The company will oversee IT project management to ensure the technology delivery schedule and integration with Hilton’s PEP PMS happens with proper execution.

"We chose techunifi LLC for their expertise, and commitment to delivering smart technology solutions," said Aziz Dhanani, CEO of Premier Petroleum Hospitality. "Their team has a proven track record of delivering complex projects on-time and within budget. We are confident that they will deliver the same level of excellence on this project." The Dual Brand Hilton Hotel is expected to be completed this summer and will feature 253 rooms, a full-service restaurant, a fitness center, and meeting rooms. techunifi LLC is proud to be a part of this exciting project and looks forward to working with a fast growing hotel developer in the Greater Atlanta market.

About techUnifi LLC techunifi LLC is a leading provider of technology solutions for businesses across various industries. The company's mission is to help organizations leverage the power of technology to improve efficiency, increase profitability, and enhance the customer experience. techUnifi LLC offers a wide range of services, including network design and implementation, cloud computing, security solutions, Hosted and on-premise PBX solutions, Pro Audio Video solutions, EV charging, ISP and SIP circuits, low voltage infrastructure solutions, and more.

03.15.24
Brew Pointe Cafe
Atlanta, GA

Brew Pointe Cafe, a startup café located near the Home2 and Tru hotels adjacent to Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport, was established on March 15, 2024. Techunifi was responsible for structured cabling, phone installation, WiFi, music, live flight information displays, and digital signage. We actively communicated with the general contractor to ensure a smooth transition to the opening. One challenge we faced was showcasing flight times for Delta passengers in the cafe. To facilitate a more seamless experience for guests, Techunifi implemented a dual-screen display solution. Working with the general contractor, CPConstruction, on this incredible project was an honor. We hope you have the chance to visit this wonderful café and enjoy its amazing ambiance. In addition to the menu boards in the café, Techunifi also helped develop the cafe's website, adding to the company’s portfolio of fully IT-managed sites.
