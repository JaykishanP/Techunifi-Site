Our Story

Techunifi's journey commenced in 2020, stemming from the visionary efforts of its founder, Rajesh Patel. Drawing upon over 25 years of rich experience within the tech industry, Rajesh meticulously crafted Techunifi with a vision to establish it as the quintessential one-stop destination for all IT solutions. With a steadfast commitment to excellence and innovation, Techunifi has seamlessly integrated itself into the fabric of the industry, serving as a trusted partner to over 75 businesses nationwide within just five years of its inception. As we set our sights on the horizon, our steadfast dedication remains unwavering; we aspire to not only sustain but elevate our trajectory, continually crafting unparalleled experiences, fostering enduring relationships, and driving forward the evolution of industry practices. Here at Techunifi, we take pride in our ability to infuse vitality into every facet of your establishment. Reach out to us today, and let us embark on this transformative journey together. We are here, dedicated to empowering your success.

Clients

Techunifi stands as a prominent figure in the realm of low voltage and telecom provision, boasting widespread recognition within the industry. Our illustrious portfolio includes esteemed names such as Hilton, Marriott, Holiday Inn, and an array of other esteemed brands. These industry giants consistently turn to Techunifi for our comprehensive suite of services and the unwavering commitment we demonstrate towards nurturing enduring client relationships. At Techunifi, we uphold the ethos of partnership at our core; we do not merely offer services, but rather, we embark on a collaborative journey with each of our clients, serving as their trusted ally in navigating the intricate landscape of IT concerns. It is this steadfast dedication to our clients' success that has propelled us into the forefront of the industry, earning us the privilege of being chosen as the preferred partner by both large enterprises and small businesses alike.
